Our story

A warm corner in the heart of Bhaktapur

✦

Nestled in the historic lanes of Radhe Radhe, Bhaktapur, Cafe Boh was born from a simple belief — that a great cup of coffee can turn an ordinary day into something extraordinary.

We source our beans with care, brew with passion, and welcome every guest like an old friend. Whether you're studying, catching up with loved ones, or simply need a quiet moment — Cafe Boh is your place.
